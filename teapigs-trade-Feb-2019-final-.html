| Matcha recipe
Ingredients
50g butter, softened*
50g caster sugar*
1 egg*
50g self-raising flour, sifted*
1 tsp matcha powder*
50ml double cream
matcha powder to dust
Steps
1. Add all starred ingredients
in to a bowl, mix well,
pour it into 2 mugs
2. Bake in a pre-heated oven
in 180C for 10-15 mins
3. Beat the cream to soft
peaks
4. Add cream onto the
cooled cake, sift matcha
powder on top
